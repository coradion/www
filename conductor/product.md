# Product Definition: Eudemonic Task Management SaaS

## Vision
To replace industrial-era task management with a "eudemonic cybernetic" system that treats human focus as a finite resource and mental well-being as the ultimate metric of success.

## Target Audience
- **Knowledge Workers:** Professionals prone to burnout and decision fatigue who need a system that protects their cognitive bandwidth.
- **High-Performance Teams:** Collaborative groups aiming to optimize their collective output without sacrificing individual mental health.

## Core Features (MVP)
- **EAS Task Capture:** A simplified input method based on the "Eliminate, Automate, Simplify" framework, minimizing cognitive friction during task entry.
- **Ergonomic UI:** A single-column, distraction-free interface built on Gestalt principles to reduce eye movement fatigue (saccades) and extraneous cognitive load.

## Value Proposition
- **Focus Protection:** Proactively shielding the user from overwhelm by managing task visibility and urgency based on cognitive load.
- **Reduced Anxiety:** Transforming the "to-do list" from a source of stress into a supportive tool that aligns with the user's mental state.

## Technical Strategy
- **Convex (Real-time State):** Utilizing Convex for deterministic, reactive state management to ensure a zero-latency user experience.
- **Google Gemini:** Leveraging Gemini's advanced semantic capabilities for intelligent task decomposition and contextual categorization.
